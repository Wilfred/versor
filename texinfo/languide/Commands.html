3 Commands

You can access Languide's commands directly with M-x, or through a keymap, or through a menu. This manual will describe the commands by their names, as used with M-x. Languide commands come in three groups, those substituting the contents of the current selection with something of the same nature, those acting on variables, expressions, and functions; and those acting on statements.

  • Alterations: Altering the selection
  • Expression handling: Expression handling, expressions, and functions
  • Statement handling: Statement handling
