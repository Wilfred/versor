9.3.1.2 Move out when at end

Default value: t Type: boolean If non-nil, trying to move further on when already at the end of the last thing in a container (see versor-allow-move-to-end-of-last will move to just after the end of the container. Can be convenient in practice, although it breaks the symmetry of the next<–>previous operations.
