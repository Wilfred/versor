4.1 Deletion

Versor defines the DEL key to delete the current selection. Note that if this is a multipart selection, such as a pair of opening and closing brackets, all parts of it are deleted. Deleting a multipart selection puts the parts of it into separate entries in the kill-ring. This is compatible with Versor's insertion commands (see Insertion) while also keeping compatibility with normal Emacs kill-ring use.
