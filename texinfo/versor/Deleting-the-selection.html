2.3.1 Deleting the selection

Pressing delete while using versor will delete the current versor selection. If it is a multipart selection, such as an opening bracket and the corresponding closing bracket (as selected by the versor “bracket nesting” dimension), all parts of the selection will be deleted, and will become successive elements of the kill ring.
